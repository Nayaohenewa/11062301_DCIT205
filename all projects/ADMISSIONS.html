Admissions Requirements for Computer Science

Interested in joining our Computer Science Department? Here's a step-by-step guide on the admissions process:

  1. Submit an application to the university. Make sure to provide accurate personal and academic information.
  2. Complete the necessary entrance exams and tests, such as SAT or ACT.
  3. Provide all required documents, such as transcripts, letters of recommendation, and personal essays.
  4. Schedule an interview with an academic advisor. This is an opportunity to ask questions and discuss your academic goals.
  5. Receive your acceptance letter from the university.

Keep in mind that each university may have different requirements and processes. Be sure to thoroughly research and consult with an academic advisor for specific information about our program.

NOTE THAT THE CUTOFF GRADE FOR BSC INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY IS AGGREGATE 12 FOR GIRLS AND 10 FOR BOYS
AND THE CUT-OFF FOR BSC COMPUTER SCIENCE IS AGGREGATE 8
